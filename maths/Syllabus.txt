DSA – Math / Number Theory Syllabus

1. GCD / LCM
   - Euclidean Algorithm (subtraction & modulo)
   - Iterative & recursive implementations
   - GCD / LCM of multiple numbers (arrays)

2. Prime Numbers
   - Prime check (optimized up to √n)
   - Sieve of Eratosthenes
   - Counting primes up to N
   - Prime factorization

3. Modular Arithmetic
   - Modular addition, subtraction, multiplication
   - Modular exponentiation
   - Handling large numbers / preventing overflow

4. Binary / Fast Exponentiation
   - Recursive & iterative binary exponentiation
   - Exponentiation under modulo

5. Factorials & Combinatorics
   - Factorials (normal & modulo)
   - nCr (combinations)
   - Pascal’s triangle

6. Bitwise Math Tricks
   - Power of 2 check (n & (n-1) == 0)
   - Odd / even checks
   - Other bit tricks for optimization

7. Advanced Number Theory (Optional / High-Level)
   - Extended Euclidean Algorithm (modular inverse)
   - Chinese Remainder Theorem (CRT)
   - Coprime checks
